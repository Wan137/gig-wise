== What it is ==

i-Saraan Plus is a voluntary contribution initiative by EPF, designed
specifically for e-hailing and p-hailing drivers. It is an enhancement of the
existing i-Saraan programme, introduced starting 2026, giving these members
the opportunity to receive larger Government incentives for retirement
security, subject to applicable terms and conditions.

i-Saraan (the general scheme) continues unchanged for other self-employed
individuals and informal-sector workers (small business owners, freelancers,
etc). i-Saraan Plus is specifically for e-hailing/p-hailing drivers and offers
a higher incentive cap than standard i-Saraan.

== Definitions ==

e-hailing: the service of transporting passengers using private vehicles,
booked through electronic mobile applications that connect drivers with
passengers needing transportation.

p-hailing: the service of delivering food, drinks, or parcels, with orders
received via mobile applications - the rider collects items from a vendor and
delivers them to a customer.

Platform Provider: an organisation or business entity that offers work
opportunities to individuals on an independent/freelance basis under a
contractual arrangement for e-hailing or p-hailing services.

== Benefits ==

Special Incentive: 20% (up to a maximum of RM600 per year) of the total
voluntary contributions made in the current year. Lifetime incentive capped at
RM6,000, or until the member reaches age 60, whichever comes first.

For existing members who already qualify for i-Saraan Plus and previously
received i-Saraan incentives, the lifetime limit is raised to RM6,000,
calculated based on incentives received since 2024. For members newly
qualifying for i-Saraan Plus, the RM6,000 lifetime limit applies to
contributions made through platform providers starting 2026.

A member can only receive one incentive in a given year - either the standard
i-Saraan incentive or the i-Saraan Plus incentive, not both simultaneously.

Annual Dividend, Death Assistance, and Tax Relief benefits are the same as
under regular i-Saraan (subject to EPF/LHDN terms and conditions).

== Eligibility ==

- Malaysian.
- Working as an e-hailing or p-hailing driver.
- Contributions must be made through a platform provider under the e-hailing
  and/or p-hailing sectors to qualify for the i-Saraan Plus incentive.
- Already an EPF member registered under i-Saraan (must register for i-Saraan
  first; enrolment into i-Saraan Plus itself is then automatic).
- Below 60 years of age.

Public service employees under a pensionable scheme, and serving or retired
military members, are eligible provided they also work as e-hailing/p-hailing
drivers and contribute through a platform provider.

== Responsibilities of platform providers ==

Platform providers in the e-hailing/p-hailing sectors are required to make
i-Saraan Plus contributions on behalf of their workers through i-Akaun
(Employer). This enables the driver to qualify for the 20% (up to RM600/year)
special incentive.

Drivers may also make additional voluntary contributions through the standard
individual channels (KWSP i-Akaun app, internet banking, bank agent counters,
registered bank agents, debit card via EPF Mobile Team/RA, or SST) to maximise
their total government matching incentive. If a driver contributes through
more than one platform provider, contributions are aggregated for incentive
calculation purposes, subject to the RM600/year cap.

== How much to contribute for the maximum incentive ==

To receive the maximum special incentive of RM600 per year, a member needs to
contribute at least RM3,000 per year under i-Saraan Plus (20% of RM3,000 =
RM600). KWSP illustration: contributing RM1,000 in the year yields RM200
incentive; contributing RM3,000 or RM5,000 both yield the capped RM600
incentive.

== How contributions are distributed across EPF accounts ==

Same distribution as regular i-Saraan:
- Akaun Persaraan (Account 1): 75%
- Akaun Sejahtera (Account 2): 15%
- Akaun Fleksibel (Account 3): 10%

For members 55 and above, contributions go entirely to Akaun Emas. The special
incentive is credited to Akaun Persaraan (Account 1) for members below 55, or
Akaun Emas for members 55 and above, twice a year (subject to receipt of the
incentive payment from the Government).

== Registration ==

A driver must first be registered for i-Saraan (via the KWSP i-Akaun app,
Self-Service Terminal, an EPF office using Form KWSP 16G (M), or i-Saraan
Online Registration). Enrolment in i-Saraan Plus itself then happens
automatically the first time a contribution is made through the driver's
platform provider - no separate registration step is required for i-Saraan
Plus.

== Important notes ==

- All i-Saraan Plus contributions paid to EPF are final - no refunds. Members
  must consent to the service provider before the contribution payment is
  made.
- Contribution limits: minimum RM1 (subject to payment channel), maximum
  RM100,000 per year combined across all EPF Voluntary Contribution channels.
